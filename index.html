I am a PhD student in Optimization and Statistical Learning at Ecole Polytechnique (CMAP), working at the french start up Safety Line. My supervisors are Frédéric Bonnans and Pierre Martinon, from INRIA Saclay. The first goal of my research is to develop a practical solution for identifying civil aircraft dynamical models using historical flights data. The second goal is to use such models to reduce future flights fuel consumption and CO2 emissions through trajectory optimization.

My main research interests are:

  • Statistical Learning: Linear model, Multi-task/Multi-output learning, feature selection, kernel methods.
  • Control theory: Optimal control, HJB, Dynamic Programming.
  • Optimization: First order methods, nonsmooth functions, nonconvex optimization.
  • Applications: Trajectory optimization, Dynamical systems identification.
