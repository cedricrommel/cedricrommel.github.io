I am a PhD student (CIFRE) in Optimization and Statistical Learning at Ecole Polytechnique (CMAP), working at the french start up Safety Line. My supervisors are Frédéric Bonnans and Pierre Martinon, from the INRIA team COMMANDS. The first goal of my research is to develop a practical solution for identifying civil aircraft dynamical models using historical flights data. The second goal is to use such models to reduce future flights fuel consumption and CO2 emissions through trajectory optimization. Before starting my PhD in November 2015, I gratuated from MINES ParisTech, where I obtained a MSc. in engineering and applied mathematics.

My main research interests are:

  • Statistical Learning: Density estimation, Functional data, Multi-task/Multi-output learning, feature selection, Linear models, kernel methods, Reinforcement Learning.
  • Control theory: Optimal control, HJB, Dynamic Programming, Dynamical systems identification.
  • Optimization: First order methods, nonsmooth functions, nonconvex optimization, alternating minimization.
  • Applications: Aircraft trajectory optimization.
